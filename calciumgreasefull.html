How to Use Spanx Calcium Grease for Maximum Protection

Step-by-Step Guide

1

Check Compatibility

Ensure the grease is suitable for your equipment and operating conditions.

2

Clean the Surface

Remove dirt, old grease, and contaminants from lubrication points.

3

Apply the Grease

Use a grease gun or manual application to apply an adequate amount.

4

Ensure Even Distribution

Move or rotate parts to ensure grease is evenly spread.

5

Check for Excess

Wipe off any excess grease to prevent buildup and contamination.

6

Reapply as Needed

Follow recommended lubrication intervals for continuous protection.

Maintenance Tips

  • ⏱️ Reapplication: Grease regularly as per equipment guidelines
  • 📊 Storage: Keep grease in a cool, dry place away from contamination
  • ⚠️ Warning Signs: Watch for excessive wear, unusual noises, or overheating
  • ♻️ Disposal: Properly dispose of old grease in accordance with regulations
