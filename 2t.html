What is 2T & 4T Oil?

  • 2T (Two-Stroke) and 4T (Four-Stroke) oils are engine lubricants designed for different types of engines.
  • 2T oil is mixed with fuel to lubricate and protect two-stroke engines.
  • 4T oil is used separately in four-stroke engines for lubrication and cooling.
  • They help reduce friction, wear, and engine deposits, improving performance.
  • Regular maintenance and oil changes ensure engine longevity and efficiency.
