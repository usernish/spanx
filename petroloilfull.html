How to Change Petrol Engine Oil in Your Vehicle

Step-by-Step Guide

1

Check Oil Type

Refer to your vehicle's owner manual for the correct engine oil grade and capacity.

2

Prepare Your Vehicle

Ensure the engine is warm, park on level ground, and engage the parking brake.

3

Drain Old Oil

Remove the oil drain plug and let the old oil drain completely into a container.

4

Replace Oil Filter

Remove the old oil filter and install a new one to ensure optimal filtration.

5

Add New Engine Oil

Pour in our premium petrol engine oil to the recommended level using a funnel.

6

Check Oil Level

Start the engine, let it run for a few minutes, then check the oil level using the dipstick. Top up if necessary.

Maintenance Tips

  • ⏱️ Change Intervals: Replace engine oil every 5,000-10,000 km (depending on manufacturer recommendations).
  • 📊 Regular Checks: Check the oil level and condition at least once a month.
  • ⚠️ Warning Signs: Watch for excessive engine noise, overheating, or dark and sludgy oil.
  • ♻️ Disposal: Properly recycle used engine oil at approved collection centers.
