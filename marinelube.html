Marine Lubricants by SPAN-X FZE

At SPAN-X FZE, we are committed to providing premium-quality marine lubricants that ensure optimal engine performance, longevity, and reliability in the most demanding marine environments. Our carefully formulated lubricants are tailored to meet the unique needs of marine engines, offering superior protection against wear, corrosion, and deposits. With our extensive range of marine oils, we ensure that your vessels are always prepared for the toughest conditions at sea.

Marine Engine Oil (SAE 30 TBN 15)

Our SAE 30 Marine Engine Oil with a TBN 15 is specifically designed for medium-speed marine engines operating under normal conditions. The TBN (Total Base Number) of 15 indicates a high alkaline reserve, helping to neutralize acids generated during combustion and prolonging the engine’s life. This oil provides exceptional wear protection, reduces friction, and enhances fuel efficiency. The SAE 30 grade ensures optimal performance in both high and low temperatures while maintaining the engine’s cleanliness by minimizing sludge formation.

  • TBN 15 for superior acid neutralization and protection.
  • Excellent wear protection and reduced friction.
  • High oxidation stability for longer oil life.
  • Low volatility and excellent emulsifying properties.
  • Ideal for medium-speed engines operating in various marine applications.

System Engine Oil (SAE 40 TBN 20)

The SAE 40 System Engine Oil with a TBN 20 is formulated for auxiliary engines in marine vessels, including generators and pumps. With a higher TBN of 20, this oil provides enhanced acid neutralization, ensuring longer oil change intervals and greater protection for engine components. Its excellent thermal stability, high load-carrying ability, and superior anti-wear properties ensure that your auxiliary systems operate efficiently in demanding conditions.

  • TBN 20 for excellent acid neutralization and extended engine life.
  • Exceptional protection against wear and corrosion.
  • Highly resistant to thermal degradation and oxidation.
  • Ideal for marine auxiliary engines, ensuring smooth operation and reliability.

Trunk Piston Engine Oil (SAE 50 TBN 70)

Our SAE 50 Trunk Piston Engine Oil with an outstanding TBN 70 is specially designed for high-output, large marine engines with trunk piston systems. The high TBN level of 70 provides superior acid neutralization, making it ideal for engines operating in harsh conditions, such as low-quality fuels or engines experiencing high sulfur content. The oil also delivers excellent deposit control, ensuring that piston rings and other critical components remain clean and efficient, thus enhancing the engine’s performance and reliability.

  • TBN 70 for superior acid neutralization, particularly in high-sulfur fuel applications.
  • Excellent deposit control to maintain engine cleanliness.
  • Outstanding protection against corrosion and wear.
  • Enhanced performance in large marine engines with trunk piston systems.
  • Ideal for long-duration voyages and high-stress operations.

Cylinder Oil (SAE 50 TBN 70)

The SAE 50 Cylinder Oil with a TBN 70 is designed specifically for two-stroke marine engines. It offers excellent protection in the high-stress environment of the combustion chamber, where extreme pressures and temperatures prevail. The high TBN level ensures effective neutralization of acidic by-products formed during the combustion process, reducing wear and maintaining the integrity of engine parts. This helps in ensuring smooth operation and minimal downtime in high-output marine systems.

  • TBN 70 for optimal acid neutralization and protection against corrosion.
  • Superior high-temperature stability and wear protection.
  • High-performance lubrication under extreme pressures.
  • Ideal for two-stroke marine engines, reducing the risk of engine failure.
