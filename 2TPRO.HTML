SPAN-X 2T Oil

SPAN-X 2T Oil is a high-quality two-stroke engine oil formulated to provide superior lubrication and performance. It ensures smooth engine operation, reduces wear and tear, and minimizes exhaust smoke. Its advanced formula helps maintain engine cleanliness and prevents carbon deposit buildup.

Performance Features

- 1. Excellent Lubrication – Ensures smooth piston movement and reduces friction-related wear.

- 2. Low Smoke Formula – Reduces exhaust emissions for cleaner combustion.

- 3. Anti-Corrosion Protection – Protects engine components from rust and deposits.

- 4. High Thermal Stability – Maintains performance in varying temperatures.

- 5. Quick Mixing – Easily blends with fuel for uniform distribution.

Applications

- Suitable for motorcycles, scooters, chainsaws, outboard motors, and other 2-stroke engines.

- Recommended for air-cooled and water-cooled two-stroke engines.

- Ideal for engines requiring pre-mixed or auto-lube systems.

Performance Standards

- Meets and exceeds API TC and JASO FC specifications.

- Complies with leading industry standards for 2-stroke engine oils.

- Recommended for engines requiring premium-grade two-stroke oil.

How to Use Spanx 2 Stroke oil

  • Check compatibility with your vehicle.
  • ✅ Use the recommended mixing ratio as per the manufacturer’s instructions.
  • ✅ For pre-mix systems: Measure and mix the oil thoroughly with fuel before filling the tank.
  • ✅ For auto-lube systems: Pour SPAN-X 2T Oil directly into the oil reservoir.
  • ✅ Ensure proper blending to avoid engine damage.
  • ✅ Start the engine and let it idle to circulate the oil properly.
  • ✅ Regularly check and maintain the correct oil levels for optimal performance.
