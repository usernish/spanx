Polymers for Industrial & Lubrication Applications

Our high-performance polymers play a crucial role in various industrial applications, including packaging, automotive components, and lubrication solutions. These polymers are engineered to withstand extreme conditions, ensuring durability, efficiency, and long-lasting performance.

Our Polymer Range:

  • ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene): Used in automotive parts, industrial applications, and oil-resistant components due to its excellent mechanical properties and high impact resistance.
  • Polyethylene (LDPE, LLDPE, HDPE): Widely used in packaging, pipes, and lubricant storage solutions due to its flexibility, chemical resistance, and durability.
  • Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET) Bottles Grade: Ideal for lubricant and oil packaging, providing excellent clarity, strength, and resistance to chemical degradation.
  • Polypropylene (Homo- & Co-polymers): Used in automotive lubricants packaging, industrial containers, and oil storage due to its high heat resistance and durability.
  • Polystyrene (GP, High Impact, E-PS): Applied in protective casing, insulation, and industrial-grade components requiring stability and lightweight properties.
  • PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride): Used in industrial pipelines, lubricant transport systems, and chemical-resistant storage solutions.
  • EPS Insulated Cups & Containers: Used in temperature-sensitive storage, ensuring stability for chemical and lubricant handling.
