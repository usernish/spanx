SPAN-X Brake Fluid DOT-3

Heavy-duty hydraulic brake fluid, such as SPAN-X Brake Fluid DOT-3, assures the extended service life of the brake fluid and braking mechanism components. It maintains its consistency and clarity in extremely cold weather, allowing braking systems to function well. Additionally, it efficiently lubricates the braking system’s moving elements.

Performance Features

- 1.High Boiling Point – Provides excellent resistance to vapor lock, ensuring consistent braking performance even under extreme temperatures.

- 2.Superior Moisture Resistance – Minimizes water absorption to maintain long-term efficiency and prevent fluid degradation.

- 3.Reduced Brake Fade – Maintains braking effectiveness under heavy loads and frequent braking conditions.

- 4.Long Fluid Life – Formulated for extended service intervals and reduced maintenance costs.

- 5.Compatible with Modern Brake Systems – Suitable for ABS, disc, and drum brake systems, ensuring reliable performance.

Applications

- Suitable for all hydraulic brake and clutch systems requiring DOT-3 specification.

- Recommended for passenger cars, motorcycles, and light trucks.

- Compatible with all standard rubber seals used in brake systems.

Performance Standards

- Meets and exceeds FMVSS No. 116 DOT-3 standards.

- Conforms to SAE J1703 and ISO 4925 specifications.

- Compatible with international automotive industry requirements.

How to Use Spanx DOT 3 Brake Fluid

  • Check compatibility with your vehicle.
  • ✅ Park on a level surface and turn off the engine.
  • ✅ Locate the brake fluid reservoir under the hood.
  • ✅ Inspect the fluid level and clean the reservoir cap before opening.
  • ✅ Pour Spanx DOT 3 Brake Fluid carefully, avoiding contamination.
  • ✅ Close the cap securely to prevent leakage.
  • ✅ Pump the brake pedal and test braking efficiency.
