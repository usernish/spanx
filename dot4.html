SPAN-X Brake Fluid DOT-4

Spanx DOT 4 Brake Fluid is a high-performance hydraulic fluid designed for use in disc and drum brake systems. It provides excellent lubrication, corrosion protection, and heat resistance, ensuring smooth braking performance and enhanced safety. Suitable for vehicles requiring DOT 4 specifications.

Performance Features of Spanx DOT 4 Brake Fluil

1.High Boiling Point – Reduces the risk of vapor lock, ensuring consistent braking performance.

2.Lubrication – Minimizes wear and extends the lifespan of brake components.

3.Superior Corrosion Protection – Prevents rust and corrosion in the braking system.

4.High Temperature Stability – Maintains efficiency under extreme heat conditions.

5.Low Viscosity – Ensures smooth operation in both high and low temperatures.

6.Compatible with Rubber Seals – Prevents leaks and maintains system integrity.

7.Meets DOT 4 Standards – Suitable for vehicles requiring DOT 4 brake fluid specifications.

Applications

1.Passenger Cars & Commercial Vehicles – Suitable for use in hydraulic brake and clutch systems of cars, trucks, and buses requiring DOT 4 brake fluid.

2.Motorcycles & Scooters – Provides reliable braking performance in two-wheelers with disc or drum brake systems.

3.Heavy-Duty & Off-Road Vehicles – Ensures effective braking under extreme driving conditions.

4.Industrial & Agricultural Equipment – Compatible with machinery using hydraulic brake systems.

5.High-Performance Vehicles – Ideal for vehicles requiring superior heat resistance and braking efficiency.

Performance Standards

SAE J1704 – Ensures high performance and reliability in hydraulic brake systems.

FMVSS 116 DOT 4 – Meets U.S. Department of Transportation (DOT) requirements for safety and efficiency.

ISO 4925 Class 4 – Certified for superior quality and performance in brake fluid applications.

JIS K2233 – Complies with Japanese Industrial Standards for brake fluids.

How to Use Spanx DOT 4 Brake Fluid for Large Vehicles

  • ✅ Ensure compatibility with heavy-duty vehicles like trucks, buses, and commercial fleets.
  • ✅ Park on a stable, level surface and switch off the engine.
  • ✅ Locate the brake fluid reservoir, typically larger in heavy vehicles.
  • ✅ Inspect the fluid level and clean the reservoir cap to prevent contamination.
  • ✅ Pour Spanx DOT 4 Brake Fluid carefully, ensuring no air bubbles enter the system.
  • ✅ Secure the reservoir cap tightly to prevent leaks in high-pressure systems.
  • ✅ Pump the brake pedal multiple times to distribute the fluid and test braking performance.
  • ✅ Regularly check brake fluid levels in fleet maintenance for optimal braking efficiency.
