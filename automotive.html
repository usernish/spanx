Automotive Lubricants by SPAN-X FZE

At SPAN-X FZE, we understand the importance of high-performance lubricants in ensuring the smooth operation and longevity of automotive engines and systems. Our range of automotive lubricants is designed to meet the rigorous demands of modern vehicles, offering superior protection, enhanced fuel efficiency, and reliable performance in all driving conditions. Whether you are driving a passenger car, a commercial vehicle, or a heavy-duty truck, SPAN-X FZE offers the right lubricant for your needs.

Our Automotive Lubricants

Motor Engine Oil SPAN-X FZE’s Motor Engine Oil is designed to meet the needs of modern gasoline engines, offering exceptional protection against wear, friction, and corrosion. Our oils are formulated with high-quality additives and base oils to provide superior performance, even under extreme operating conditions. Available in a range of API-approved and SAE grades, including SAE 10W-30, SAE 5W-40, and more, our engine oils ensure smooth engine operation, reduced emissions, and extended engine life.

API Certification: API SN, API SM, API SL

SAE Grades Available: SAE 10W-30, SAE 5W-40, SAE 15W-40, SAE 20W-50. Fully Synthetic Options Available: For enhanced performance and longer oil change intervals.

Diesel Engine Oil: Our Diesel Engine Oil is specially formulated for high-performance diesel engines, ensuring they operate efficiently under heavy load and high-stress conditions. SPAN-O FZE diesel engine oils meet the stringent requirements of modern diesel engines, providing superior protection against soot, wear, and oxidation. These oils are available in API CK-4 and API CI-4 ratings, making them suitable for a wide range of diesel-powered vehicles, from light-duty trucks to heavy-duty applications.

API Certification: API CK-4, API CI-4, API CJ-4

SAE Grades Available: SAE 15W-40, SAE 10W-30, SAE 5W-40. Fully Synthetic and Semi-Synthetic Options Available: For increased durability and performance.

Automatic Transmission Fluid (ATF): SPAN-X FZE’s Automatic Transmission Fluid (ATF) is engineered to ensure smooth shifting, optimal transmission performance, and protection against wear in automatic transmission systems. Whether for passenger vehicles, commercial fleets, or heavy machinery, our ATF offers superior anti-wear and anti-foaming properties, as well as excellent friction control for seamless gear changes. Our ATF fluids meet Dexron III, Mercon V, and other OEM specifications.

API Certification: Dexron III, Mercon V, JASO MA2, and more

Brake Fluid: SPAN-X FZE’s Brake Fluid ensures the highest level of safety and reliability for your vehicle’s braking system. Our brake fluids are formulated with premium additives that offer outstanding corrosion protection, high-temperature stability, and superior water absorption to maintain optimal braking performance. Suitable for both conventional and performance vehicles, our brake fluids meet DOT 3, DOT 4, and DOT 5 specifications.
