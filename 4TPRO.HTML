SPAN-X 4T Oil

SPAN-X 4T Oil is a premium four-stroke engine oil designed to deliver superior protection and performance for motorcycles and other 4-stroke engines. It ensures smooth engine operation, reduces wear and friction, and enhances fuel efficiency. The advanced formula helps maintain engine cleanliness and longevity.

Performance Features

- 1. Superior Engine Protection – Reduces wear and extends engine life.

- 2. Enhanced Thermal Stability – Performs well in high-temperature conditions.

- 3. Anti-Friction Formula – Minimizes friction for smooth gear shifting.

- 4. Advanced Detergency – Keeps the engine clean and prevents deposits.

- 5. Optimal Viscosity – Ensures proper lubrication across all engine speeds.

Applications

- Suitable for motorcycles, scooters, ATVs, and other 4-stroke engines.

- Recommended for air-cooled and liquid-cooled four-stroke engines.

- Ideal for high-performance and commuter motorcycles.

Performance Standards

- Meets and exceeds API SL and JASO MA2 specifications.

- Provides excellent protection for wet clutch systems.

- Recommended for engines requiring high-quality four-stroke oil.

How to Use Spanx 4T Engine Oil

  • ✅ Check compatibility with your vehicle and refer to the manufacturer’s guidelines.
  • ✅ Park on a level surface and turn off the engine before changing the oil.
  • ✅ Drain the old oil completely before refilling with SPAN-X 4T Oil.
  • ✅ Use the recommended oil grade and quantity specified in your owner's manual.
  • ✅ Securely close the oil filler cap to prevent leaks.
  • ✅ Start the engine and let it run for a few minutes to circulate the oil properly.
  • ✅ Regularly check and maintain the oil level for optimal performance.
